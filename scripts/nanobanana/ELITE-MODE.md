# TACO CITY — Briefs Nano Banana para MODO ELITE

> Flujo: estos briefs se pasan a **Nano Banana (Gemini Image)** para generar mockups/arte ELITE.
> Luego el código (canvas) debe quedar **idéntico** al mockup (diseño + estructura + vista), igual que
> el flujo de CleanPro-ERP. Generá cada uno en 16:9 y 9:16 (desktop y celular).
>
> Estilo global a respetar en TODOS: 3D estilizado tipo Crash/Roblox amigable, materiales con volumen,
> iluminación cinematográfica (key + rim light), paleta alegre (amarillos #f5cd46, cyan #7fd6ff,
> verdes #60d050, magenta #e0479a, dorado #ffd84a), sombras suaves, post cine (viñeta + grano sutil +
> barras letterbox). El protagonista es SIEMPRE un TACO humanoide (tortilla amarilla doblada con
> relleno de lechuga/tomate/queso, ojos grandes, bracitos y piecitos). No usar marcas registradas.

---

## 1. Key art / Título ELITE
```
Cinematic key art of "TACO CITY", a friendly 3D action game. Hero: a cute humanoid TACO character
(folded yellow tortilla shell with lettuce/tomato/cheese filling, big expressive eyes, little arms and
feet) striking a heroic pose in the foreground, wearing a frog bucket hat. Behind him, a vibrant
stylized city at golden hour with chunky rounded buildings, glowing windows, a shopping mall sign, and
a swirling purple portal. Volumetric god rays, rim light, soft shadows, bloom, subtle film grain,
cinematic letterbox bars. Big bold playful logo "TACO CITY" in chunky 3D letters. Bright joyful palette
(yellow, cyan, green, magenta, gold). Mobile game cover quality, 16:9.
```

## 2. Ciudad ELITE (isométrica 2.5D)
```
Isometric 2.5D stylized city block for a cute action game, "Crash Bandicoot meets Monument Valley"
vibe. Chunky extruded buildings with 3 clearly shaded faces (light roof, mid side, dark side), glowing
warm windows, rooftop AC units and water tanks, striped shop awnings with readable signs (MODA, CAFE,
ARCADE), asphalt streets with painted lane lines and crosswalks, concrete sidewalks, flower planters,
trees, street lamps glowing, small colorful cars. Diamond isometric grid, soft ambient occlusion, rim
light, gentle bloom, cheerful daylight. A humanoid taco character walking on the street for scale.
Clean readable shapes, high contrast, mobile-friendly. 16:9 and 9:16.
```

## 3. Mall ELITE (interior, 4 pisos / atrio)

Interior of a bright modern shopping mall, stylized 3D, isometric-ish. Multi-level atrium with a glass
skylight pouring light, balcony railings on the upper floor, a big animated escalator in the center,
rows of glowing storefronts with striped awnings and signs (MODA, JUGUETES, CAFE, ARCADE), mannequins

## 4. Taco héroe ELITE (turnaround + outfits)

Character turnaround sheet of a cute humanoid TACO hero (folded yellow tortilla shell, lettuce/tomato/
cheese filling, big eyes, little arms and feet, gloss highlight), front/side/back, 3D stylized, soft
studio lighting, rim light. Include cosmetic variants in a grid: frog bucket hat, sunglasses, superhero

hat + reflective safety vest), and riding mounts (roller skates, scooter, bicycle). Consistent

## 5. Combate ELITE (escena de acción)

Dynamic action shot: the humanoid taco hero blasting "salsa" projectiles with a comedic bazooka at a

## 6. HUD / UI ELITE
```
Mobile game HUD UI kit, stylized and chunky, dark glassy panels with neon accents (cyan, gold, magenta),
rounded buttons, a circular twin-stick control, health bar, score counter, weapon chip, power-up timers,
a minimap, a mission banner, and a friendly little AI assistant robot avatar with a speech bubble.

```

## Cómo integrarlo (cuando tengas las imágenes)
1. Generá cada brief en Nano Banana (16:9 + 9:16). Guardá en `scripts/nanobanana/output/elite/`.
2. Usalas como REFERENCIA para igualar el render del canvas (no como assets cargados, para mantener el
   juego sin dependencias) — o, si querés, las cargamos como fondos/sprites raster en una rama "elite".
3. El **MODO ELITE in-game** (Opciones → MODO ELITE) ya aplica el grado de color + viñeta + grano +
   letterbox cinematográfico sobre el render vectorial, para acercarse al look de estos mockups.
